GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform): A JavaScript library for creating animations on websites.

gsap.to(): Animates an element from its initial state to a defined final state.

gsap.from(): Animates an element from a defined final state to its initial state.

Stagger: Executes animations on multiple elements one after another for dynamic effects.

Timeline: Synchronizes multiple animations for precise control over sequencing and timing.

Yoyo: Creates animations that oscillate back and forth between initial and final states for fluid motion.
